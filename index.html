What makes this different

No nursery, no neighbor,
no one has this exact tree.

Most Japanese maples are grafted clones — hundreds of identical plants sharing the same genetics. Ours are grown from seed. That means the maple you choose from us is a biological original: its color, form, and seasonal character belong to it alone.

A hundred years from now, it could be the most distinctive tree on your street. Japanese maples grown this way live for generations — improving, deepening, becoming more themselves with every passing season. That's worth planting for.
